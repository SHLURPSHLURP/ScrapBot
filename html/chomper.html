Chomper

Discovered by ScrapBot in Episode 2- First Steps

Chomper was originally a demolition bot, designed to break down scrap metal and chew through debris. Built like a miniature walking bomb casing, it was programmed to bite through metal, process materials, and assist in wrecking operations. However, when the world fell silent, Chomper was left buried under a collapsed structure, its systems running on emergency power for years until ScrapBot found it. Most of its original directives are corrupted, but one instinct remains intact: Bite first, think later.

Buffs:

  • >Strength: Chomper's ingrained instinct to fight means there is a 60% chance ScrapBot won't have to fight.
  • >Curiosity: Chomper's lack of fear results in it digging around unfamiliar places. 70% chance to bring back 2 extra scraps to the warehouse.
