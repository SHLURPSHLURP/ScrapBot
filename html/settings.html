About the game: ScrapBot

After waking up in post apocalyptic world where there are no more humans, a lone and damaged robot finds its way to an abandoned warehouse. The robot collects scraps found in the warehouse to fix itself and thus names itself "ScrapBot". After many days feeling desolate and alone, ScrapBot ventures out of the warehouse to gather more scraps to build companions.

Venture out of the warehouse and complete episodes and chapters to gather scraps. Use these scraps in the workshop to build decorations and more friends.
